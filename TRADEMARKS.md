## Requirements for forks and redistributed builds

Before distributing, publishing, hosting, or making a fork or derivative work available to others, remove or replace all reserved branding, including:

- application names and titles that use reserved names
- logo, icon, and favicon files
- embedded UI assets
- documentation branding
- package metadata
- executable and installer names, where they use reserved branding
- website, support, and marketing references that imply affiliation

## Limited factual references

You may make limited factual references to the upstream project only when needed to preserve required copyright, attribution, and license notices, or to accurately describe the origin of the unmodified upstream work. These references must be accurate, non-promotional, and must not be used as part of the derivative product's own identity.
